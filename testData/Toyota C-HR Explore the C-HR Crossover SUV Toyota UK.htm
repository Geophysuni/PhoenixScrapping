LEAVE ORDINARY BEHIND

In a world of ordinary, stand out with the Toyota C-HR's angular dynamic design; powerful performance and agility.

If you’re a business user, calculate your company car tax here.

  • The Toyota C-HR has been designed with the city in mind. Sharp, responsive acceleration is combined with nimble handling and manoeuvrability to give you control in tight spaces and the ability to go with the urban flow.

  • The Toyota C-HR is an efficient self-charging hybrid which switches effortlessly between electric energy or a combination of petrol and electric power.

  • The race track keeps inspiring the design of our cars, just like the Toyota C-HR GR Sport- Its sharp, dynamic style is destined to take your breath away. Eye-catching details like the striking 19” alloy wheels and piano black wheel arches reinforce the athletic credentials of this striking Toyota C-HR.
