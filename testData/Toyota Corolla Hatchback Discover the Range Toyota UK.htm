DRIVING SATISFACTION

With its eye catching design, excellent handling and improved performance, the latest safety and multimedia systems with powerful connectivity and live Over-The-Air updates, the new Corolla Hatchback is ready to make every moment special.

  • The Corolla brings a comfortable and natural drive with our 5th generation hybrid system, precision steering and suspension, along with a rigid body and low centre of gravity. These come together to provide exceptional handling and stability.

  • The Corolla has a distinctive and strikingly sleek exterior, which can be made even more eye-catching with a variety of bi-tone paint options. A low bonnet increases the drivers field of vision while an impressive wide grill and stand out LED headlights add to the refined profile.

  • The racetrack keeps inspiring the design of our cars, just like the Corolla GR SPORT – it’s sharp, dynamic style is destined to take your breath away. Eye-catching details like the 18” alloy wheels, side sills with black inserts & sports seats, reinforce the athletic credential of this Corolla.

    TOYOTA SAFETY FEATURES

    Toyota Corolla Hatchback is built with Toyota Safety Sense – your helpful driving partner. This includes the latest generation of Pre-collision and driver assist systems as well as the possibility to update its features over-the-air.
    • Toyota Safety Sense Improvements in the operation and functionality of the systems include, Acceleration Suppression, which reacts to the sudden use of the accelerator when travelling at low speed; collision avoidance support when making a turn at an intersection; Emergency Steering Assist; lateral collision avoidance and curve speed reduction when using adaptive cruise control. Over-the-air software updates will keep the functions up-to-date and enable new ones to be added.
    • Driving assistance Our Blind Spot Monitor system* alerts the driver to any vehicles they might not have seen in their side mirrors. This is particularly useful when overtaking. Corolla Hatchback also features Emergency E-call and Adaptive High-Beam system.
    • Parking assistance Additionally, Corolla Hatchback is equipped with other innovative assistance features such as Safe Exit Assist*, Rear Seat Reminder System and Rear Cross Traffic Alert* with automatic braking to name just a few- giving you confidence and creating an even safer driving environment for you and other road users.
    *Standard on Excel grade
